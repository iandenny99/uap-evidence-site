Contribute

Help expand the evidence library responsibly. We prioritise primary sources and clear distinction between fact, claim, and critique.

What to send

  • Links to primary documents (official records, transcripts, FOIA releases, datasets).
  • Links to peer-reviewed papers or formal studies.
  • Clear timeline (dates, locations, people) and why it matters.
  • Known critiques / counter-claims with citations.

Submission checklist

  • Have you included original URLs (and archive links if available)?
  • Can a journalist verify your claims independently from the links provided?
  • Is personal data redacted where appropriate?
  • Is your summary neutral and concise (2–4 sentences)?
