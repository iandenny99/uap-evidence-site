Panorama Pitch: Secrets in the Sky

This is a working proposal for a Panorama investigation into UAP (Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena). It sketches how a documentary could follow the evidence: primary documents, sworn testimony, and on-record interviews—then test competing claims with the BBC’s standards of proof.

Important note: This is an editorial outline, not a finished script. Some roles are placeholders pending further reporting (for example, precisely who blocked specific UAP Disclosure Act provisions at each stage). All assertions would require independent BBC verification against the primary sources linked across this site.

If you have relevant information, corrections, or leads, please share them. And if you’d like Panorama to take this forward, use the button below to email the team with your own short introduction.
